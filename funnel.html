❓ Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my school's data safe and secure? +
Yes. EduStack PK uses enterprise-grade encryption for all data at rest and in transit. Your school data is stored on dedicated cloud servers, never shared, and protected with regular backups. Only your authorized staff can access your school's data.
Do I need to install any software or app? +
No installation needed. EduStack PK is a web-based platform — it works on any device with a browser. Teachers can mark attendance on their phone, accountants manage fees on a laptop, and you can monitor everything from anywhere.
What happens after the free trial ends? +
You choose a plan and continue without any disruption. All your data, students, and settings stay exactly as they are. If you decide not to continue, we export all your data for you — no lock-in, no data loss.
Does it work for colleges teaching Intermediate (Part I & II)? +
Absolutely. EduStack PK is built specifically for Pakistani private schools and colleges, including Intermediate Part I & II grading. The result module automatically applies the correct grading system based on class level.
Can I manage multiple campuses or branches? +
Yes. From the Growth plan onward, you can manage multiple branches from a single login. Each branch has its own staff, students, and data, but you can see reports and analytics across all branches in one place.
Will you train our staff on how to use it? +
Yes. Every new school gets a free onboarding session where we walk your team through the platform. We also provide video tutorials and a WhatsApp support group for quick questions. Most staff are comfortable within 2–3 days.
